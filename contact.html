Reasons to reach out

We genuinely want to hear from readers. The library is a living resource and we update it as evidence evolves. Here are the kinds of messages we prioritize:

Research corrections
You spotted an error, an outdated study citation, or a pair where newer evidence points a different direction.
Missing pairs
There is a nutrient synergy with published human research that we have not yet covered. Tell us what and why.
Product suggestions
You know a brand with excellent form, third-party testing, and transparent labeling that we should evaluate.
General feedback
Something on the site is confusing, broken, or could be explained more clearly. We appreciate it.
